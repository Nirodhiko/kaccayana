Sattehi kilesehi ca saṅgaṇanaṃ samodhānaṃ saṅgaṇikā, sā āramitabbaṭṭhena ārāmo etassāti saṅgaṇikārāmo, tassa. Tenāha ‘‘gaṇasaṅgaṇikāya cevā’’tiādi. Āraddhavīriyassāti paggahitavīriyassa. Tañca kho upadhiviveke ninnatāvasena ‘‘ayaṃ dhammo’’ti vacanato. Esa nayo itaresupi. Vivaṭṭanissitaṃyeva hi samādhānaṃ idhādhippetaṃ, tathā paññāpi. Kammassa-katapaññāya hi ṭhito kammavasena bhavesu nānappakāro anatthoti jānanto kammakkhayakaraṃ ñāṇaṃ abhipattheti, tadatthañca ussāhaṃ karoti. Mānādayo sattasantānaṃ

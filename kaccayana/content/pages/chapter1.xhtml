Tatiyassa paṭhamadutiyesu natthi vattabbaṃ. Tatiye ‘‘hatthago’’ti vattabbe ‘‘hatthako’’ti vuttaṃ. So hi rājapurisānaṃ hatthato yakkhassa hatthaṃ, yakkhassa hatthato bhagavato hatthaṃ , bhagavato hatthato puna rājapurisānaṃ hatthaṃ gatattā nāmato hatthako āḷavakoti jāto. Tenāha ‘‘āḷavakayakkhassa hatthato hatthehi sampaṭicchitattā hatthakoti laddhanāmo rājakumāro’’ti. Catutthādīni uttānatthāneva.
